About LocoBuddy

The life of a college student is so hectic that it is very important for him/her to stay in a good environment after college hours to help them focus. The major problem faced by the students getting admission in colleges is finding the perfect place to crash in which is: near to the college, in a safe locality, having facilities like 24x7 water, electricity, cleaning help, gas pipeline, furniture and the list goes on. In one word, a home away from home. Also being in a packed schedule, it is not possible for many students to cook, therefore an addition to the list is good places to eat nearby.

We aim to bridge the gap between the flat owners, pg owners,brokers, restaurants, that deal with providing accomodation to stay,and food to eat with those students who have newly taken admission in our college, are completely new to the city, with hostels being full, are looking for the perfect place to settle, hoping good food to be nearby. We connect the vacant flat/pg owners with the students by bringing them together and make the flat/pg finding process easier and faster.

What makes LocoBuddy different is that we not only care for the accomodation of the students, but also keep in mind the struggle of these students to find food to kill their hunger. Thus we provide a option for the flat/pg owners as well as restaurant owners as to post the description of the place along with photos.The locations of these places and restaurants can be easily viewed by our students on the map available and in-turn book the pickup slots at the same time.
